Make a Paper Flower: Easy Origami Flower Instructions for Kids

HOME ABOUT ORIGAMI

MATERIALS REQUIRED

1. Origami Paper

2. Colour Pen

STEP1:

Start with a square piece of origami paper with color side down, but if you only have regular 8.5x11 paper, follow these instructions to make a square sheet.

STEP2:

Fold the paper into a triangle by folding the bottom corner to the top corner.

STEP3:

Fold the triangle into a diamond by folding the left and right corners to the top.

STEP4:

Take the flaps you just folded and fold them outward to the left and right edges of the diamond

STEP5:

Unfold the left side and insert a finger into the pocket of that flap, then squash down as shown.

STEP6:

Repeat step 5 for the right side as well so that you have two squashed sides.
