Make a Paper bird: Easy Origami bird Instructions for Kids

HOME ABOUT ORIGAMI

MATERIALS REQUIRED

1.a Coloured paper

2.coloured pen

STEP1:

Get out your favorite origami paper. If you only have regular 8.5x11 paper, follow these instructions to make a square sheet.

STEP2:

Fold the left corner to the right corner. Make sure it's straight down the middle.

STEP3:

Unfold the paper so we can have the crease in the middle.

STEP4:

Fold the lower half of both sides to line up with the center.

STEP5:

Fold the lower of half of both sides to the center again.

STEP6:

Turn the figure over.

STEP7:

Fold the bottom corner to the top corner. Don't worry about the dark color in the picture, it's just the shadow.

STEP8:

Do this squash fold three more times to the other flaps until you're figure looks exactly like this.
