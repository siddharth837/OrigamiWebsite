Make a Aeroplane:Easy Origami Aeroplane Instructions for Kids

HOME ABOUT ORIGAMI

MATERIALS REQUIRED

1. Origami Paper

2. Colour Pen

STEP1:

Start with a regular piece of rectangle paper that's 8.5 x 11.0 inches

STEP2:

Fold the left half to right to make a crease in the center.

STEP3:

Unfold.

STEP4:

Fold both top corners to the center.

STEP5:

Fold the new left and right corners to the center.

STEP6:

Fold plane in half from right to left.

STEP7:

Now fold the left edge to the right to form the wing. Use your fingernail to help make the tip as pointy as possible.

STEP8:

Turn the plane over.

STEP9:

Fold other wing to line up with the first wing.

STEP10:

Unfold the wings.

STEP11:

Make sure the wings are tilted up a bit.
