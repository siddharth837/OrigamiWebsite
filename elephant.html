Make a Paper elephant: Easy Origami Dog Instructions for Kids

HOME ABOUT ORIGAMI

MATERIALS REQUIRED

1.a square sheet of paper

2.scissors

3.a piece of ribbon, wire or thread

STEP1:

Start with a square piece of origami paper.

STEP2:

Fold paper in half by folding the top corner to the bottom corner.

STEP3:

Let's create a crease in the middle. Fold in half again by folding left corner to right corner, and then unfold.

STEP4:

Now fold the left and right edges of the triangle to the center crease.

STEP5:

Fold both top corners to the side.

STEP6:

Fold the left and right corners in like this.

STEP7:

Turn the figure over.

STEP8:

Fold up the elephant's trunk and fold in back down like this.

STEP9:

Keep folding the trunk up and down like an accordian.

STEP10:

Pull out the elephants trunk and add some eyes.
